ABOUT ME!

My name is Lina and I am from Colombia. Currently I am a student of Interactive Design & Technology at Saskpolytech.

Why landscapes?

I have a passion for landscapes. By shooting landscapes you can appreciate how beautiful and perfect the world is. Landscape photography pushed me to explore new places. It is super therapeutic, you can literally transport yourself to that place.

Why a YouTube Channel about Canada?

2 years ago I decided to start a YouTube channel because I am obsessed with Canada. When I came to Canada my friends always asked me, How do you live in the cold weather? How are the landscapes? How is life over there? Canada is a country where everyone around the world would dream of living in. It is a country that has all the opportunities for people of all races, languages and cultures. Returning to my YouTube channel, my channel is in Spanish and it's dedicated to show content about Canada. I have not yet dared to speak in English since it is my second language and I do not feel 100% confident. However, eventually I will have to do it in English to allow me to reach more people around the world.
